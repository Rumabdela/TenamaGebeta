Meet Our Team of Experts

Our team combines deep knowledge of traditional Ethiopian culinary practices with modern nutritional science to provide you with the most accurate and helpful information.

Dr. Alem Gebre

PhD in Nutritional Science

Dr. Alem is a leading researcher in the field of indigenous Ethiopian crops and their health benefits. With over 15 years of experience, his work focuses on the nutritional composition of grains like **teff** and **sorghum**, as well as the benefits of traditional fermentation methods. He is passionate about bringing ancient wisdom into modern dietary practices.

Teff Nutrition Fermented Foods Plant-Based Diets Agroecology

Nutritionist Selam Fesseha

Clinical Nutrition Specialist

Selam has a deep understanding of how to use Ethiopian cuisine as a therapeutic tool. She works with clients to create personalized meal plans using traditional foods to manage and prevent chronic conditions such as **diabetes**, **hypertension**, and **obesity**. Her approach emphasizes balanced, wholesome eating rooted in cultural heritage.

Diabetes Management Heart Health Weight Management Therapeutic Nutrition

Chef Yordanos Hailu

Culinary Nutrition Expert

Chef Yordanos is a celebrated chef who is committed to making healthy Ethiopian cooking accessible to everyone. She develops and tests our recipes, ensuring they are not only authentic and delicious but also nutritionally sound. Her expertise lies in modernizing traditional dishes to be lighter and more suitable for contemporary lifestyles without losing their rich flavor or heritage.

Traditional Cooking Recipe Development Healthy Modifications Food Preservation
